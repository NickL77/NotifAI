Messaging Program Overview

NotifAI is a personal notification service operated by Nicholas Liu. It sends low-volume conversational SMS reminders, notifications, and test messages from NotifAI to the owner and a small group of friends who have directly consented to receive them. Messages may be generated or assisted by a large language model.

Messages are not promotional or marketing messages. NotifAI does not send third-party advertisements, affiliate marketing, lead generation messages, or messages on behalf of unrelated businesses.

SMS Consent and Call to Action

Recipients opt in by texting START to the NotifAI phone number after receiving or being shown the NotifAI SMS consent disclosure.

The opt-in disclosure is: "Text START to opt in to receive conversational SMS reminders, notifications, and test messages from NotifAI. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase."

After texting START, recipients receive this opt-in confirmation: "NotifAI: You are now opted in to conversational SMS reminders, notifications, and test messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out."
